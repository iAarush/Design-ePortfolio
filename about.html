About me

Hey everyone! I am a 10th grade student at Oakridge International School, Bangalore. I am passionate about global issues such as education, climate change, and food waste. I created this project with the hope of making my school more sustainable.

About my product

My product is a composter that will take food waste produced in the cafetaria and produce compost that can be used on the school's greens.

A video of my product will be uploaded on or before March 6, 2020.

About my client

My client is Oakridge International School, an IB school in Bangalore and a part of the Nord Anglia family.
